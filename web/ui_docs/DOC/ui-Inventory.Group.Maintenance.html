Schedule Downtime*

This page allows you to specify a period of time during which metric collection for the resources in a group is paused. During the interval, alerts that are active for the resources will not fire. The Schedule Downtime page is available on the Tools menu when a group is selected.

Feedback is welcome. Click Add Comment at the bottom of the page.

To schedule maintenance:

  1. Navigate to the group for which you which to schedule downtime.
  2. Choose Schedule Maintenance from the Tools menu. 
  3. On the Schedule Downtime page, enter the start and end dates and times for the maintenance window and click Schedule.

You can reschedule the maintenance period by following the procedure described above, changing the maintenance window, and clicking Reschedule.
